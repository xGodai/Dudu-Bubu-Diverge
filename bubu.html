Bubu!

The ADHD Wizard!

Bubu: The ADHD Wizard is a vibrant and magical story about a young spellcaster named Bubu, a fearless wizard with ADHD whose fast-paced mind and boundless energy become her greatest gifts. Fueled by curiosity, creativity, and heart, Bubu navigates a world full of distractions and challenges, using her unique thinking to solve problems, spark wonder, and defend what matters most. This empowering tale celebrates neurodiversity, imagination, and the magic of embracing how your mind truly works.

Bubu Powers:

Hyperfocus Spell

When inspiration strikes, Bubu becomes unstoppable.
She uses bursts of deep concentration to master powerful spells and complete quests in record time—before the magic fades.

Quick-Think Dash

Fast mind, faster moves.
Bubu’s rapid thinking lets her react instantly in chaotic situations, turning impulsivity into quick and clever spellcasting.

Energy Burst

Too much energy? Perfect for big magic.
Instead of sitting still, Bubu channels her restlessness into movement-based magic, fueling powerful spells with her boundless energy.

Chaos Compass

Lost in distractions? Not Bubu.
She uses enchanted tools—like magical checklists and timers—to stay on track and guide her wild mind through complex missions.

Creative Storm

Rules? Bubu bends them with flair.
Her out-of-the-box thinking helps her invent new spells, solve problems creatively, and surprise even the wisest wizards.

Heart of Fire

Big feelings mean big passion.
Bubu’s emotions may come fast and strong, but they fuel her empathy, courage, and fierce loyalty to her friends and cause.
